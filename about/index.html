Welcome to osu!UWaterloo!

osu!UWaterloo welcomes anyone interested in osu! regardless of skill level, gamemode, or however else they may choose to enjoy osu! We run a number of events each term including:

  • Waterloo osu! Cup
  • osu!-related contests
  • Trivia
  • In-person meetups

No term is the same, but you can always expect fun and unique events!

How to Join

  1. Join the Discord Server.
  2. Fill out out the Verification Form.
  3. Ping the Club Executive role in the #verification channel.
  4. Participate in events!

Club Meetings

osu!UWaterloo officially meets in-person bi-weekly, usually on Saturday 2-6pm at PHYS 145. Please check the #announcements channel in the Discord server to stay up-to-date!

Collegiate Teams

Players who wish to compete at a competitive level are encouraged to play for the osu!UWaterloo collegiate teams. These teams participate in osu! Collegiate League (o!CL) which runs every fall and winter term, with standard, mania, and taiko gamemodes. The osu!std league runs both a Tier 1 and Tier 2 tournament side-by-side, so players of all skill levels are encouraged to try out!

Please contact @pikapwn on Discord if you are interested in competing.
